Cultural Sprint 1

Identity, Values and Strengths Exploration

Using the evidence gathered, describe your core personal values.

Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made and the consequences of the action you took.

I had a decision to make when I got made redundant during my maternity leave. I was treated unfairly while being away from the organisation on maternity leave and wasnt given the chance to stand up for my role while they did a restructure since I had been away from the organisation for 6 months. I decided that I did not agree with how they treated myself and other woman in the organisation so when I was offered other positions to apply for I decline that offer as I decided it went against my values to work for an organisation that treats me and other people that way. It was a hard decision to weigh up as leaving the company meant that we did not have my income to rely on for when I was due back but for myself and my husband it wasnt worth the extra money to go against my values. The decision I made was to leave the job. The consequences of this action was that I no longer had a role to return to but this was a good thing as it lead me to push myself to do what I had been wanting to do for a while which was to study web development.


Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity.

I have grown up with a very hardworking and determined background. Both my parents were/are very hard working and determined people and I believe that this has been passed down to me. The value of manaakitanga has always been strong in my culture too. To treat other people with kindness and respect. I always try to follow this value and incorporate in my daily life.


Using the evidence gathered, describe your strengths and limitations.

Evaluate your strengths and limitations in terms of your learning and career development

My strengths would be my determine and motivation to succeed. Another strength would be my willingness to give anything a go and to keep trying when things get tough. My limitations are the personal juggle that I will have to do to balance family life with two small kids and a husband and getting the learning and career development completed. I know that my determination and motivation to successfully complete this course and then move on to become a web developer will definitely help me to succeed. This motivation and seeing the end goal will help to ensure that I can get past any learning obstacles that come my way. Share an example from your experience of where you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss the strategies you used at that time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now, and why.


Share an example from your experience of where you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss the strategies you used at that time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now, and why.

In my previous job my manager would believe that her ideas were always correct and would struggle to take on other peoples ideas if they werent in line with her own ideas. This caused tension and led to a not very cohesive team and also stifled any forward momentum in the team as well as she had very old school ways of doing things. The strategies I used at the time were to present my ideas to her in a clear and concise way with plenty of evidence backing them up so that she would struggle to pick apart the idea. I would also compromise and bring in her ideas into my ideas so that it wasnt a huge change for her and that she could still feel included while we still gained forward momentum. This was sometimes effective but other times she did not budge from her original ideas.Other strategies I would try would be to get the support of my whole team and approach it together with the support of everyone. I would look and see the different learning styles and personality styles of the people I am working with and learn about how it is best to approach each personality style e.g some people are better with plans and written information while other people like seeing stuff get done first and then explaining it to them afterward.
