Cultural Sprint 2

Learning Plan

LONG TERM GOAL

My long term goal is to get a job as a software developer. For many years I have sacrificed my career for other aspects of life and ended up in jobs that I havent been satisfied in and also jobs that I havent been appreciated in. My goal is to get a job that is flexible and gives me that job satisfaction.

SKILLS TO DEVELOP

Organisation skills. Not so much my life organisation as I feel like I am good at that. But organising the smaller details like folders and emails.

STRENGTHS AND LIMITATIONS

My strengths would have to be my motivation to achieve this goal. I have both my own self to achieve for as well as my family so my motivation level is definitely very high. Another strength is my time management skills. Having two young kids and already fitting in two hours of study a day as well as general household chores has made me learn to be very good at time management. Another strength is my support network. My husband is fantastic. Not only is he as excited as me for me to be doing this course but he has also taken on extra tasks around the house and will be taking on the majority of child wrangling during the bootcamp. He is also a great support as he is a software developer himself so is there to help if I have any questions but also encourages me to push myself and is helping me set myself up for the future career. E.g he wont just tell me the answer but help me realise how to google for it myself etc. My limitations is my organisational skills when it comes to the finer details like organiser folders on a computer or emails etc. Another limitation which can also be a strength is that I am not a perfectionist often I will be satisfied to get things to MVP and then I am ready to move on. I believe this can definitely be a limitation but can also see that it is a strength. Another of my limitations is that I often get a bit put off if I get stuck on a problem. I have been working very hard lately to make sure that I power through until I find a solution.

COMMITMENTS

I am commited to completing this course and gaining as much technical and core skills as I possibly can.

If I come across a problem I cant solve. I will seek help from my own cohort to see if anyone else knows the answer. This will help everyone else learn as well and help if someone else has a similar problem to me. I will also seek help from the facilitation team. I will also seek help from my husband. And I will also google the answer to get used to that way of working.

I look forward to having the support and passion from the faciliation team both towards myself and my learning path and also towards my fellow cohort.

SCHEDULING.

For the foundation course I have my daughter at home with me all day so I tend to do study in the evenings or her lunch nap. During the weekend I usually spend most of my time with the family but my husband will take the kids for a few hours during the day so I can get some uninterrupted study. Then also at night as well. I realise juggling the kids and this course will be one of my biggest challenges but with the support from my husband and family and friends I believe I can do it. I am away in Rarotonga from the 14–18th September. I will make sure that I am ahead of my workload before I head away for these 5 days.


During the bootcamp I would love to still make it home occasionally to see the kids before bedtime but myself and my husband do realise that this will not happen every night and that some weekends will be taken up as well.
